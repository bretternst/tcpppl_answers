Exercise 2 defines the graphics library and the classes that go with it.

Exercise 3 adds support for line thickness and style.

Exercise 4 adds support for line arrowheads.

Exercise 5 prevents drawing outside the bounds of the surface (even though this was already supported
by the graphics subsystem).

Exercise 6 adds support for drawing text.

Exercise 7 adds the ability to connect two shapes via their closest connect points.

Exercise 8 adds color: backround, shape outline (stroke) and closed shape inside (fill).
